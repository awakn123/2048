How to play: Use your arrow keys to move the tiles. When two tiles with the same number touch, they merge into one!

This project represents the culmination of CS5100, Foundations of Artificial Intelligence, a course offered at the Northeastern University Roux Institute, under the guidance of Jonathan Mwaura.

Developed by Yun Cao and Jitong Zou, this work builds upon the principles of the popular game '2048'. We extend our gratitude to Gabriele Cirulli, the creator of the original version, for their invaluable contribution.
